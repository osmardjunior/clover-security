Understanding Data Security

As a business you need to stay on top of your data security. We’ve compiled a set of content to help your learn about the important aspects of data security.

What Is Data Security?

Data Security refers to the protections and safeguards you put in place to protect your data from the risk of it being lost, stolen, corrupted or used for inappropriate purposes or without permission.

What Is Cyber Security?

As data is largely stored electronically in today’s world, cyber security refers to the protections you put in place to protect your systems, networks, programs, devices and data from being compromised by a hacker or cybercriminal in the form of a cyber attack. Cyber attacks vary widely and can range from simply slowing down your business device(s), to stealing or corrupting your data to completely shutting down access to all files on your system.
